📝 Past Tense in Narrative Texts

Innarrative text (fairy tales, legends, fables, and even science fiction), the use of thepast tense is very important because they recount events that have already happened..

🔹 1. Simple Past Tense

Formula: (+) S + V2 | (–) S + did not + V1 | (?) Did + S + V1 ?

🏰 Fairy tales:
• Once upon a time, there was a little girl.
• She met a wolf on her way.

🚀 Sci-fi:
• The spaceship landed on a strange planet.
• The scientist discovered a new source of energy.

🔹 2. Past Continuous Tense

Formula: (+) S + was/were + V-ing | (–) S + was/were not + V-ing | (?) Was/Were + S + V-ing ?

🏰 Fairy tales:
• The children were playing when the giant appeared.
• While the princess was sleeping, the witch entered the castle.

🚀 Sci-fi:
• The robot was exploring when it found a strange signal.

🔹 3. Past Perfect Tense

Formula: (+) S + had + V3 | (–) S + had not + V3 | (?) Had + S + V3 ?

🏰 Fairy tales:
• The fairy had disappeared before the prince arrived.

🚀 Sci-fi:
• The scientist had completed his experiment before the machine exploded.

🔹 4. Past Perfect Continuous (opsional)

Formula: (+) S + had been + V-ing | (–) S + had not been + V-ing | (?) Had + S + been + V-ing ?

🏰 Fairy tales:
• He had been wandering in the forest for days before he found the stone.

🚀 Sci-fi:
• The crew had been working for hours before they fixed the spaceship.

📊 Ringkasan Tense

Tense Fungsi Contoh
Simple Past Alur utama cerita She found a key.
Past Continuous Suasana / kejadian yang sedang berlangsung She was walking when she saw a door.
Past Perfect Kejadian yang sudah lebih dulu The wizard had stolen the light.
Past Perfect Continuous Durasi sebelum peristiwa lain They had been working for hours.

📖 Mini Cerita Gabungan

Once upon a time, a young boy found (Simple Past) a key in the forest. He was walking (Past Continuous) along the river when he saw a shining door. The kingdom he entered had been suffering (Past Perfect Continuous) from darkness for years. The people told him that the wizard had stolen (Past Perfect) the light long ago. Finally, the boy saved (Simple Past) the kingdom.

👉 Simple Past = cerita utama, Past Continuous = suasana, Past Perfect = peristiwa duluan, Past Perfect Continuous = durasi.
