Listening Competencies

  • Understand the main ideas in spoken science fiction stories.
  • Identify specific information from audio or video texts.
  • Develop the ability to take notes while listening to sci-fi narratives.
  • Respond accurately to questions based on listening materials.

Speaking Competencies

  • Retell a science fiction story orally in a coherent way.
  • Role-play characters from science fiction narratives.
  • Express ideas, opinions, and predictions related to sci-fi texts.
  • Present short dialogues or mini-presentations on sci-fi themes.
