Listening Indicators

  • Students can identify the main idea of a science fiction audio text.
  • Students can catch specific details (characters, setting, events) from listening material.
  • Students can respond correctly to comprehension questions after listening.
  • Students can take short notes while listening to a sci-fi story.

Speaking Indicators

  • Students can retell a short science fiction story using their own words.
  • Students can role-play as characters from a science fiction narrative.
  • Students can express personal opinions and predictions about a sci-fi story.
  • Students can deliver a short oral presentation on a science fiction theme.
