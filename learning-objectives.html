Objectives of the Lesson

After completing this lesson, students will be able to:

  • Understand the purpose and scope of narrative texts in science fiction.
  • Develop reading, listening, speaking, and writing skills through sci-fi materials.
  • Identify the generic structure and language features of narrative texts.
  • Improve vocabulary mastery related to science and futuristic themes.
  • Enhance critical and creative thinking by analyzing and creating sci-fi stories.
